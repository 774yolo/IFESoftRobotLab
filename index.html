Welcome to IFE Soft Robot Lab

We are an interdisciplinary team that seeks to develop multifunctional flexible/soft electronics to address important scientific and technical challenges in soft robots. Our current research focuses on advanced soft electronic devices for efficient sensing, communicating, and computing that adapt to highly elastic bodies of soft robots, pushing the boundary of their multifunctionality for dynamic, uncertain tasks.

Affiliations

We are affiliated with the Institute of Flexible Electronics (IFE), Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), China.
